# Shadow Threads: Occult Urban Fantasy

Welcome to a version of our world where the skin of reality is stretched to the extreme, letting the horrors stirring beneath show through. **Shadow Threads** is a universe where the concrete of metropolises hides cathedrals of flesh and where secrets are not whispered, they are screamed.

### 1. The Veil and Corruption
The reality we know is nothing but an illusion, a fragile weaving intended to protect the human mind from the unbearable truth. Behind this **Veil**, ancient entities and entropic forces seek to tear the threads to invite themselves into our world.
In this universe, magic is not a divine gift, but a **Corruption**. Using supernatural threads is like manipulating barbed wire: the more you pull on it to get what you want, the deeper it sinks into your own flesh and your humanity.

### 2. Moods and Influences
The game sits at the crossroads of occult investigation and visceral horror:

* **Carnal Horror:** The body is a malleable material; scars, mutations, and pain are the bargaining chips of power.
* **Urban Corruption:** The city is a living organism, often sick, where every alley can lead to a liminal space or a forgotten cult.
* **Transformation:** The central theme is the loss of self. By dint of "Probing the Threads" or "Braving the Supernatural Danger," the character irreparably moves away from their human nature.

### 3. The Three Levels of Reality
Depending on your campaign, you will choose the characters' level of involvement with the Veil:

| Level | Character Profile | Health Mode |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Realistic** | Ordinary humans (cops, journalists) with no supernatural abilities. Geniuses (d12) but strictly human. | **Realistic** (1 light box) |
| **Normal** | Initiates who have pierced the Veil. They possess occult knowledge and minor abilities beyond the human spectrum. | **Normal** (3 light boxes) |
| **Heroic** | Hybrid beings or members of powerful factions acting on both sides of the Veil. Their humanity is already compromised. | **Heroic** (5 light boxes) |

### 4. Glossary of Shadows

* **The Abyss:** What lies behind the Veil. A space of chaos and impossible shapes.
* **Initiates:** Those who have seen the truth and can no longer turn back.
* **The Flesh:** Term used to designate physical reality, often considered a prison by the entities of the Abyss.
* **The Great Weaving:** The very structure of reality, whose threads can be knotted or cut by the Fates.
