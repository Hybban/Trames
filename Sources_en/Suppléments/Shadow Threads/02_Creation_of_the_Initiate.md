# II. Creation of the Initiate

The creation of your character depends on the **Reality Level** chosen for your chronicle. This level determines not only your physical resistance, but also the nature of your traits and your relationship with the Fates.

### 1. REALISTIC Mode: The Blind
*The world is gray, cold, and rational. You know nothing of the Veil until it tears open before you.*

* **Archetypes (d12):** Concrete social functions. E.g.: *Homicide Detective, Investigative Journalist, Medical Examiner, History Student.*
* **Traits:** Only human abilities. A d12 represents excellence or genius in a conventional field.
* **The Loom of the Fates:**
    * **Clotho (Conviction):** Earthly values. E.g.: "The truth must come out," "Protect my family."
    * **Lachesis (Destiny):** A mundane life goal. E.g.: "Get justice for my brother," "Become editor-in-chief."
    * **Atropos (Sin):** A human flaw. E.g.: *Alcoholism, Consuming ambition, Cowardice.*
* **Resources:** Professional equipment, savings, legal contact network.

### 2. NORMAL Mode: The Initiate
*You have seen the Abyss. You can no longer turn back. You have acquired knowledge that humanity rejects.*

* **Archetypes (d12):** Roles on the border of the Veil. E.g.: *Street Occultist, Relic Hunter, Haunted Medium, Freelance Exorcist.*
* **Traits:** Mix of human skills and occult knowledge (e.g., "Knowledge of demons," "Ritual of protection").
* **The Loom of the Fates:**
    * **Clotho (Conviction):** Linked to the secret. E.g.: "Contain the Abyss," "Learn the name of things."
    * **Lachesis (Destiny):** A mystical quest. E.g.: "Find the lost grimoire," "Close the rift in my neighborhood."
    * **Atropos (Sin):** An attraction to the shadow. E.g.: *Morbid curiosity, Mystical pride, Need for forbidden sensations.*
* **Resources:** Minor artifacts, contacts in the occult underworld, access to secret archives.

### 3. HEROIC Mode: The Defector
*You are no longer entirely human. You carry within you the mark of the Abyss or the allegiance of a Veil faction.*

* **Archetypes (d12):** Hybrid or powerful beings. E.g.: *Cult Enforcer, Limbo Hybrid, Blood Sorcerer, Guardian of the Threshold.*
* **Traits:** Manifest supernatural abilities (e.g., "Shadow manipulation," "Inhuman strength," "Vision of the beyond").
* **The Loom of the Fates:**
    * **Clotho (Conviction):** Non-human principles. E.g.: "Maintain the balance of the threads," "The flesh is a prison to break."
    * **Lachesis (Destiny):** A prophecy or ascension. E.g.: "Take the Master's place," "Provoke the awakening of the city."
    * **Atropos (Sin):** Active corruption. E.g.: *Bloodlust, Metaphysical sadism, Contempt for human life.*
* **Resources:** Cursed artifacts, protected sanctuaries, supernatural servants or allies.

---

### Summary of Creation Differences

| Element | REALISTIC | NORMAL | HEROIC |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Magical Traits** | Forbidden | Knowledge & Rituals | Manifest Powers |
| **Resources** | Mundane | Rare / Strange | Cursed / Powerful |
| **Human Limits** | Strict | Porous | Surpassed |
